# biot-savart-toolkit
Files and tools to help simulate Biot-Savart Integral induced flow in various solitons

Comprised of research for the Mines Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship (SURF) 2025 and research done in collaboration with Dr. Scott Strong (Colorado School of Mines AMS)
- results disseminated in Mines Undergraduate Fall Symposium 2025 (poster included)

BSI_integral from "Non-Hamiltonian Kelvin wave generation on vortices in Bose-Einstein condensates," Scott A. Strong and Lincoln D. Carr (2018)
